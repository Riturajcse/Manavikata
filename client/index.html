Welcome to Manavikata

Manavikata is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO), registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 (Regn.No- 7123/2017). Founded in 2017 by Mr Sandip Roy, Manavikata undertakes various social activities by helping the poor and the needy. We believe that the purpose of human life is to serve, show empathy and have the will to help others. We are committed to reach out to each and every deprived people of Tripura. If you are willing to serve God by serving the poor, the aged and the helpless, we welcome you to be a part of our organisation. You can become our volunteer and participate in our activities, financially donate for our old age home 'Pather Sathi', donate food for feeding the hungry, register with us for blood donation, provide free tuitions or donate clothes to poor students and spread the message of our humanitarian activities through social media. You can extend your helping hand to bring smiles to millions of faces and happiness in millions of hearts. Come, join us and be the change you wish to see in this world.
